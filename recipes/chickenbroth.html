Chicken Broth

Simple Chicken Bone Broth

Behold! A simple recipe for Chicken Bone Broth that anyone can follow! This recipe produces a simple, yet highly nourishing, broth that you can add to any dish or enjoy on its own.

There's nothing quite like warm chicken soup when you're under the weather. Chicken broth is the base of many dishes across the globe. Learning to make this staple is a great foundation for great cooking.

Ingredients

  • A Whole Chicken
  • Water
  • 4 Tbsp Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Turmeric
  • 2 Tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2 Whole Onions Chopped (4 Tbsp Powdered)
  • 3-5 Cloves of Garlic Chopped (3 Tbsp Powdered)
  • 3 Large Carrots with Skin
  • 3-4 Stalks of Celery
  • 1 Bunch of Cilantro Chopped
